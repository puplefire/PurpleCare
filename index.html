Frequently Asked Questions

What is the role of a pharmacy technician?

Pharmacy technicians assist pharmacists in dispensing medications, managing inventory, and interacting with patients.

Do I need a license to work as a pharmacy tech?

Yes, most regions require certification or registration with a pharmacy board.

How do I prepare for the Pharmacy Technician Certification Exam (PTCE)?

Study official guides, take practice tests, and join discussion forums like PurpleCare!
